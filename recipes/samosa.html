Samosa

Description

Learn how to make delicious gujarati samosas at home. It is a popular Indian deep fried snack made with spicy potato and lentil fillings.

  • 2 large potatoes
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • vegetable oil
    • <1i>1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  1. First, boil water.
  2. Then add cumin seeds and peas and leave for 5 mins to boil
  3. After that, add potatoes
  4. Now our samosas are done.
